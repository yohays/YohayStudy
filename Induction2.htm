Induction

 

Learning Outcome:

1.       Provide an introduction to KOL and the University of Essex Online, its virtual learning environment (VLE), Moodle, and the available library resources.

2.       Familiarize students with the resources available in the Study Skills Hub.

3.       Guide students on how to best engage in online learning (learning communities, organizing learning).

4.       Introduce students to the ideas of reflective learning and critical thinking.

5.       Refresh knowledge of referencing and the avoidance of academic dishonesty.

6.       Introduce the skills required for successful completion of future modules (including academic writing and research skills).
